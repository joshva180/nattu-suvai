      Frequently Asked Questions

      • Is Zoho Payroll a part of Zoho One?

        Yes, Zoho Payroll is included in Zoho One. Reach out to our support team (support@zohoone.com) to understand how the pricing and licenses are handled.

      • Are there any feature differences between the standalone Zoho Payroll and the Zoho One version?

        No, Zoho One includes all features of the single-state payroll plan (Standard) of Zoho Payroll.

      • How can I access Zoho Payroll within Zoho One?

        Zoho Payroll is a part of the Finance module in Zoho One. Once you’ve purchased Zoho One user licences, you can access Zoho Payroll and process payroll for your employees.

      • How many users can I add to Zoho Payroll with my Zoho One licenses?

        If you have 10 user licenses in Zoho One, you can add up to 10 users (users and employees) with unique email addresses in Zoho Payroll. To add more users, you’ll need to purchase additional employee licenses in Zoho One or contact support@zohoone.com to discuss your requirements.

      • Can I try the product before purchasing it?

        Absolutely. Sign up for 14 days free-trial and enjoy unrestricted access to all the features of Zoho Payroll. No credit card information required.

      • I am convinced by Zoho Payroll. What are my payment options?

        We accept payments via Visa, MasterCard, and American Express.

      • Do you store my credit card information?

        No, we don't store your credit card information. Read our Privacy Policy to learn more.

      • How secured are my transactions?

        We secure your payroll data with 256-bit SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, regular intrusion/virus detection and prevention scanning.

      • Can I run more than one business?

        Yes, absolutely. You can have multiple businesses as different organizations associated with your Zoho Payroll account.

      • Have more questions?

        We are available 24 hours a day from Monday to Friday. Email us at support.usa@zohopayroll.com or call us at 8443165544

      • Are there any additional charges outside the subscription cost?

        For some services like instant wire transfer, yes. Read this document to know in detail about all the other services that come at an additional price.
